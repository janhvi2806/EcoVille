Here are a few ways that you can make your makeup more eco-friendly:


1) Search for more sustainable packing options, so that you can recycle some of the packaging of your makeup.

2) Read labels. It can be tricky to identify when something is fake since companies do a great job advertising something as "natural".

3) Use all of your makeup. Don't waste it!

4) Look for eco-friendly makeup wipes. There are a lot of bamboo ones on Amazon.

5) DIY: Try making your own makeup! Have you heard of crayon lipstick?
