Remember, reduce, reuse, and recycle.

1) Reduce: Buy less clothes, but buy the ones that are better quality.

2) Repair: Try fixing broken clothes instead of getting more over and over again

3) Try buying eco-friendly clothing that are organic, recycled, or made out of materials like bamboo, linen, etc.

4) If you're in the mood, organize a clothing swap with your friends to wear new clothes without buying them.


Please consider using some of the strategies we provided!
