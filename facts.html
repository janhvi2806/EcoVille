Our environment is one of the biggest things through which we thrive and grow. We depend on the environment for many things. Without protecting and taking care of our environment we're putting so many lives at danger such as animals, plants and crops, and even our own. We've listed some facts about the environment to explain just how much the environment is changing and just how important it is to protect the environment.


1) In the last 170 years, we added 2.4 trillion tons of Carbon Dioxide into our atmosphere. Half of this was added in the last 35 to 50 years.

2) The world uses 160,000 plastic bags every second.

3) Plastic is Forever. It doesn’t bio-grade and go back to nature. It photo-degrades into smaller pieces of itself, continually polluting the environment throughout its life cycle.

4) Global temperatures have increased by about 1° Celsius in the past century.

5) As global temperatures increase, our societies will find it harder to adapt to the changes this brings, and some species are more likely to go extinct.

6) If the entire world’s Ice melted, our sea levels will rise by 66 meters.

7) The 20 warmest years on record have been in the past 22 years.

8) 11% of the world's population is vulnerable to climate change impacts currently.

9) 120,000 square kilometres of tropical forest were lost in 2018.

10) More than 1 million species are at risk of extinction by climate change.
